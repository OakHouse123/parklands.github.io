Welcome to Parklands

Our care home, situated on the outskirts of Bradninch overlooking the Devon countryside, provides a tranquil and welcoming setting for the elderly looking for physical, mental and social care. With a warm, home-from-home atmosphere, we offer compassionate, personalised care tailored to each resident's needs.

Our Approach to Care

At Parklands, our approach to care is centred on understanding the unique needs of each individual. Our team focuses on dignity, routine, and meaningful engagement, creating a calm and reassuring atmosphere where residents feel truly at home.

The House

Our tranquil Parklands home is a spacious, detached property overlooking the rolling Devon countryside. Nestled on the outskirts of the Duchy town of Bradninch, it offers a peaceful rural setting ideal for rest and wellbeing. This historic town provides a strong sense of community and calm, while being just a short drive from Exeter. The home features 18 tastefully decorated bedrooms, designed to create a warm, home-from-home atmosphere for our residents.
